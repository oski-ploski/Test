About Us

Clipper Bardon DC, part of Clipper Logistics plc, is a state-of-the-art warehouse facility located in Ellistown, Coalville. We specialize in providing comprehensive logistics and supply chain solutions, with a significant focus on e-commerce and store replenishment services.

Our Services

  • E-commerce Fulfillment: Efficient handling and distribution of online orders.
  • Store Replenishment: Timely and accurate supply of goods to retail locations.
  • Contract Logistics: Tailored logistics services to meet diverse client needs.
